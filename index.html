Vitez Book – new novel by A.A.

Vitez is an upcoming novel by A.A. A dark historical and philosophical story about identity, fate and inner conflict, intertwined with a centuries-long search for eternal love. Through different eras and hidden truths, the novel explores secret societies, ancient mysteries, and the price of immortality. The official website of the book Vitez presents the cover, selected quotes, and first visual materials before release.

Languages planned: Croatian, English, German, Korean.

Keywords: Vitez book, Vitez novel, Vitez A.A., Croatian novel, historical mystery novel, secret societies, eternal love story, philosophical novel, new book coming soon.

The word "Vitez" means knight – Ritter (German), Chevalier (French), Caballero (Spanish), Cavaliere (Italian), Rycerz (Polish), 騎士 (Japanese), 기사 (Korean), 骑士 (Chinese). The novel explores the eternal archetype of the knight through history, love, secret societies and destiny.
